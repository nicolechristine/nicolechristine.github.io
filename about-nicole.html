there is no lover and no

beloved, there is only love and
truth expressing and flowing
within itself."

Nicole Christine

Nicole Christine was born in Germany in 1973. Her first contact with the Dharma was as a child, when she naturally began to meditate on her own. After earning an MA, she had a successful career, and saw her material wishes fulfilled. Despite this, she still felt a deep inner sense of not being really happy and missing something in her life, until she was strongly drawn to visit Mount Kailash in Tibet. There she underwent a profound transformative experience which deepened her dedication to the Dharma and for meditation.


After having spent many years in silence and intense practice, she was asked by her teachers if she would help others on their spiritual path. Since then, she has been sharing her experience and understanding of the path in talks, silent retreats, and one-to-one meetings. Nicole is a certified practitioner of Craniosacral therapy, which she sometimes uses, together with energy treatments, to support the unique process of transformation in others.


While having a deep understanding of the sacred literature of many traditions, Nicole prefers to share from her own direct experience. She delights in sharing the light of truth and love, and in helping others on their unique journey towards liberation. Her approach is one of openness and love for people from all backgrounds.
